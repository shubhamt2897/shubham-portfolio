LeHarvester

Imitation learning SO-101 ACT + SmolVLA

I co-developed an autonomous fruit-harvesting prototype using the LeRobot SO-101 leader-follower setup, custom cutting hardware, dual-camera demonstrations, and learned manipulation policies.

01
Teleoperation to dataset I recorded multimodal demonstrations with front and wrist cameras plus proprioceptive state through LeRobot.
02
Policy training and rollout I trained ACT and SmolVLA paths with Hugging Face and AWS workflows, then deployed policies on the live arm.
03
Hardware and telemetry I integrated custom gripper hardware, CAD assets, ESP32 hall-sensor telemetry, dashboard monitoring, and Telegram alerts.

Hierarchical Manufacturing Agent

Scheduling Vision inspection LeRobot ACT

I built a full-stack production scheduling agent for PCB manufacturing that connects ERP orders, operator approval, camera inspection, defect handling, and physical robot sorting.

01
Planning under production constraints I read Arke sales orders, resolve priority/deadline conflicts with EDF, and schedule seven production phases.
02
Human-in-the-loop control I use Telegram plus Gemini 2.5 Flash to approve, reject, reorder, delay, and inspect schedules with natural language.
03
Inspection-gated robot execution I gate SO-101 ACT execution with EfficientNet-B0 classification; defects trigger reject trajectories and rework orders.
